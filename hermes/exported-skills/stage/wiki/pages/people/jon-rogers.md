# Jon Rogers

## Summary

Jon Rogers provided feedback on the MCEU project, emphasizing lore consistency, gamified exploration, overcoming Loot's shortcomings with text NFTs, dynamic AI visuals tied to story progression, and the promise of ERC-6651 for equipable NFT attributes.
