# Story App Initiative Three-Phase Strategy

## Summary

A three-phase strategy to accelerate product velocity and build toward a larger platform outcome: Phase 1 launches first-party apps for validation, Phase 2 productizes shared infrastructure into an API/SDK, and Phase 3 builds an App Store-style ecosystem layer.
