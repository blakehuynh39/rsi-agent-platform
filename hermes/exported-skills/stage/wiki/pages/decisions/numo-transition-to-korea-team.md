# Numo Transition Plan

## Summary

The Numo project ownership is transitioning to the Korea team with a target production-ready app by June 30, 2026. The Korea team will lead mobile app development, growth hacking, and Toss Mini App strategy. An intensive onboarding runs June 3-10, after which the Korea team assumes primary ownership. Shared infrastructure around user identity and reputation will be jointly developed.
