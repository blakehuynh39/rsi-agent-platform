# Subnet 2.0 Rewards and Incentivization

## Summary

Outlines the reward and incentivization strategy for Subnet 2.0, including initial Poseidon token grants for subnet owners that gradually decrease, shifting revenue to data buyers and sequencer fees.

## Claims
