# Numo Launch Day Metrics and Terminology Decision

## Summary

During Numo's launch day, the afternoon digest showed 6,314 submissions with 6,322 pending review. The team observed rapid growth, reaching 10,000 submissions within hours. A terminology discussion led to an agreement to use 'task submissions' in graphics, 'submissions' in writing where context is established, and 'contributions' for broader narrative. The team also decided to delete a previous day's graphic in favor of the more impressive 10k milestone.
