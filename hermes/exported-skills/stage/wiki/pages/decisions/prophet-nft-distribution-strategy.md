# Prophet NFT Distribution and Wallet Strategy

## Summary

The team evaluated airdrop vs minting for Prophet NFTs, leaning toward a simple minting page with airdrop fallback for non-web3 users. Educational wallet resources and a create-wallet backup feature were also proposed.
