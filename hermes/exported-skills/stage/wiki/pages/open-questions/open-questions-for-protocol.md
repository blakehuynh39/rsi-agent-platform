## Open Questions

- Do we need a Linking Module for story-story linking?
- How does licensing work and what assumptions simplify it?
- How to handle art (fan art, canonical representations, different styles)?
- How to handle canon and promotion to canon?
- What are the permissions and payments models?
- What is the atomic unit of a story and how to reconcile stories with collectible copies?
- What modules need to be built for White Fountain and what assets do they apply to?
- Whether to continue using collections?
