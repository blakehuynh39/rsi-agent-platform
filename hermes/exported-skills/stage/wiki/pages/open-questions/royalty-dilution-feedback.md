# Royalty Dilution Feedback

## Summary

Feedback from Mahojin and others requesting dilution mechanism for royalty percentages to prevent burden on descendants, and counter arguments from the team about vulnerabilities.

## Claims

- Mahojin suggests that royalty percentages should dilute over generations, e.g., if C earns 100, B gets 10 and A gets 1, rather than fixed percentages that accumulate. `claim:claim_1_1` `confidence:1.00`

- IPX and Twitter also made similar requests around non-fixed allocations of royalty tokens. `claim:claim_1_2` `confidence:1.00`
