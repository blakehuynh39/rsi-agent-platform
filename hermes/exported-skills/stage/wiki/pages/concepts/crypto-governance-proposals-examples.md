# Crypto Governance Proposals and Voting Examples

## Summary

Examples of governance proposals and voting mechanisms across Cosmos (on-chain), Compound (Tally), and Balancer (Snapshot), highlighting the trade-offs between on-chain execution and off-chain signaling.

## Claims

- Off-chain voting (Snapshot) is flexible, allows lower-risk experimentation with voting strategies, is free, and quicker/cheaper to set up.
