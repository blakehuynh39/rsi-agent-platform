# Ecosystem Growth Strategy

## Summary

A strategy for growing the RSI ecosystem by first focusing on fanfiction for existing hit franchises, then expanding outward. Emphasizes composability between franchises and uses the 'Bobby Hundreds' as a potential focal point.
