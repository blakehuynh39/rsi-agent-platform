# Indian Speech Data Collection Opportunity

## Summary

An unmet need exists for in-situ, code-switched, multi-speaker, environment-tagged, commercially licensed conversational audio data across Indian languages. Current ASR models fail on Indian speech (OpenAI 55%+ WER), and no commercial data exists to fix it. A time-bound window of 6-12 months exists before competitors (Voice of India, Luel) occupy the market. Existing 80,000+ hours are scripted/clean-room/telephonic. Two collection tracks (app-based young urban bilinguals, field-based shopkeepers/service workers) are necessary. Compliance/consent provenance is a competitive moat.
