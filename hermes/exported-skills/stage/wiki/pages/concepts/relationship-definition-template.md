# Relationship Definition Template

## Summary

A standardized template for proposing and defining relationship types (links) between IP assets in Story Protocol, inspired by Ethereum Improvement Proposals. It includes fields for name, scope, source/destination eligibility, criteria, and pre-action hooks to coordinate community and development efforts.
