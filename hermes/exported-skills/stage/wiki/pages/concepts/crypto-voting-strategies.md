# Crypto Voting Strategies and Types

## Summary

Overview of voting strategies used in crypto governance, including token-based voting power calculations, voting types, and quorum/threshold concepts.

## Claims

- Voting power is usually calculated using tokens (ERC20, ERC721, ERC1155) or combinations. Strategies can involve governance tokens, staked tokens, LP tokens, vested/locked tokens, community badges, or NFTs. `claim:claim_2_1` `confidence:1.00`
