## Summary

Snapshot of IP token allocation and flow from the Aeneid genesis as of 2026-06-09. 1.02B IP distributed across 8 EOAs, with 520M untouched and 500M moved by three operational wallets. Key findings: C holds ~123M IP mostly staked; A holds ~189M liquid; F gave away its 100M genesis via transfers and stakeOnBehalf. Staking contract ownership rules detailed separately.
