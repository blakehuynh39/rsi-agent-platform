# Contributor Payout and Status Multipliers

## Summary

Proposed reward system where campaign payouts vary by data modality demand, and contributors earn maintainable status multipliers to boost retention.

## Claims

- Campaign payouts should be higher for in-demand data modalities. For example, Hindi audio pays more than Korean audio, free-form audio more than transcript audio, video more than audio.
