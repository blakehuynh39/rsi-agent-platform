# Design Goals of the Programmable Blockchain Framework

## Summary

The design goals for the programmable blockchain framework include programmability via an interface definition DSL, scalability, security, support for different transportation layers, interchangeable use of geth or reth, and separation of consensus and execution clients (local or remote).
