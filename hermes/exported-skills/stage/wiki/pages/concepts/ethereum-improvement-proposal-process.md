# Ethereum Improvement Proposal (EIP) Process

## Summary

Ethereum Improvement Proposals (EIPs) define standards for the Ethereum platform, including core protocol specs, APIs, and contract standards. They follow a defined lifecycle and process modeled after BIP and PEP.
