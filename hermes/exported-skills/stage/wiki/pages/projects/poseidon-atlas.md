# Poseidon Atlas Proposal

## Summary

Atlas is a proposed demand-side marketplace within Poseidon that curates a vetted, multilingual, desktop-native pool of domain experts for AI evaluation and annotation tasks. It aims to productize the highest-value human evaluation layer, starting with audio validation and expanding to professional task review, computer-use capture, and on-demand expert data.
