# Royalty Vault Auto-Deployment for Non-Commercial IPAs

## Summary

Project to deploy royalty vaults automatically for IPAs lacking commercial licensing, enabling Verio's staking and tipping rewards to flow into a royalty vault. Workarounds exist, and the full integration is targeted for the next Portal major release in June.
