# Internal Agentic Initiative

## Summary

An initiative under the Lion Team, focused on AI-native company proposals and current agentic efforts including AI Agent Factory, Security Bot, Web3 AI Auditor, Pentesting Tool, and Web3 Alert Triager. It also covers standardization, safe defaults, skill libraries, and a standardized agent harness.
