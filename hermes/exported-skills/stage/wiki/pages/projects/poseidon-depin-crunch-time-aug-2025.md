# Poseidon DePIN Crunch Time, Aug 2025

## Summary

Two-week crunch time (Aug 4-18, 2025) to release the Poseidon DePIN MVP on World Appstore, a batch data pipeline for rare voice data, and the first version of the Lite Paper.
