# Kleros

## Summary

Kleros is a decentralized dispute resolution protocol. It has processed over 1700 disputes since 2018, currently handling 7-10 cases per week with a maximum capacity of 1000 cases per month after some changes. The protocol operates v1 on Mainnet and Gnosis, with a v2 planned for July featuring cross-chain capabilities and Proof of Skills. Dispute fees range from $15 to $50 USD depending on juror count and required skills.
