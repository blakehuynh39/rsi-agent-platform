# Two-month plan

## Summary

The two-month plan for the Tiger Team outlines assignments and goals for building DePIN apps and ecosystem foundation. Month 1 aims to ship 3 usable first-party apps with shared infrastructure. Month 2 aims to create an ecosystem foundation with app-store shell, submission flow, and hardened onchain/privacy rails.
