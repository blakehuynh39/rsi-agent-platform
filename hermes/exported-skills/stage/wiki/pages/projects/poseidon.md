# Poseidon

## Summary

Poseidon is an internal project aiming to build a reusable storage layer to replace IPFS on Story L1, with a focus on privacy protection and a production service in 4 months. The 0th internal release is targeted for May 21, 2025, with a permissioned design and no POS token.
