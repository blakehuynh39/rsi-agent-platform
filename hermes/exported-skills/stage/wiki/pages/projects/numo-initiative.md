# Numo Initiative

## Summary

Numo is a collaborative initiative between supply side (Poseidon) and demand side (Story), using a shared Slack channel for transparent communication to align on collection goals, task design, and audio validation timelines starting with Bengali. Validation timeline directly affects user monetary incentives, and a key lesson from Season 1 is not to promise rewards without concrete demand. A product/sync call was scheduled for April 22, 2026, and initial channel members include several Slack users.
