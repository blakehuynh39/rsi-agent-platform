# Dispute Module Improvements

## Summary

The dispute module requires significant work to standardize information collection, accusation categories, and the appeals process. It must handle multiple dispute categories across different modules (registration, license, royalty) without creating conflicts between the PIL dispute process and other module dispute processes. Common copyright-based dispute categories include piracy, unauthorized usage, attribution, moderation, and payment. During a dispute, multiple parties may be notified and certain actions (royalties, licensing, registration) may be paused.
