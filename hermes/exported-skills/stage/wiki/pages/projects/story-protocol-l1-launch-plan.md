# Story Protocol L1 Launch Plan

## Summary

Engineering plans for Story Protocol L1, covering launch items, precompiles, proof-of-creativity, custom data types, partners, 1st-party apps, cross-chain, token design, staking, and a phased timeline with internal devnet (SIDN) targeting June 17 and partner devnet (SPDN) targeting July 15.
