# Numo Expert Project

## Summary

Numo Expert brings the internal Poseidon annotation workflow into app.numolabs.ai, enabling qualified users to complete audio/transcript annotation tasks with quality controls, consensus, honeypots, onboarding, and payout tracking. The project is planned in phases: parity with internal tool, contractor beta, permissionless onboarding with quizzes and consensus, and later mobile/deepfake modalities.
