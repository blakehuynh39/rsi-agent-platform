# L1 Feature Research List

## Summary

A rough list of features that could be researched or developed on the L1 blockchain, including networking improvements (libp2p, erasure encoding, HTTP/3, gossipsub) and consensus enhancements (HotStuff 2+, fast BLS signature aggregation, 2s block time).
