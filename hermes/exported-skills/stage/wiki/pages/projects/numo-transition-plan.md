# Numo Transition Plan

## Summary

Ownership of Numo transitions to the Korea team with a target of delivering a production-ready mobile app by June 30, 2026. Focus includes mobile app development, growth hacking, and the Toss Mini App strategy. Shared user identity, reputation, and trust infrastructure will continue to be developed collaboratively across teams.
