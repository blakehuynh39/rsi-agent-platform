# Numo Transition Plan & Korea Team Goals

## Summary

As of June 2026, the Numo project ownership transitions to the Korea team. The plan outlines a target delivery of a production-ready app by June 30, 2026, with phased onboarding and primary ownership periods. The Korea team will also lead growth initiatives and the Toss Mini App strategy. Post-handoff, collaboration continues on shared identity and reputation infrastructure.
