# Numo App Strategy

## Summary

Numo is a two-sided marketplace that rewards users for data and sells packaged data to AI labs. The strategy focuses on phased launches: first casting a wide net for user volume, then rating users and finding quality via Adaptive Trust Sampling, and finally crowdsourcing data QA and building an SDK ecosystem.
