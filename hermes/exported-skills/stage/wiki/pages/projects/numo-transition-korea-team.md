# Numo Transition Plan & Korea Team Goals

## Summary

The Numo project ownership transitions to the Korea team, targeting a production-ready app launch by June 30, 2026, with intensive onboarding June 3-10 and primary ownership June 11-30. Post-launch, both teams collaborate on shared reputation/identity infrastructure.
