# Numo Transition to Korea Team

## Summary

Ownership of the Numo project transitions to the Korea team, with a target production-ready app by June 30, 2026. Intensive onboarding week (June 3-10), primary ownership from June 11-30, post-launch collaboration on shared identity/reputation infrastructure.
