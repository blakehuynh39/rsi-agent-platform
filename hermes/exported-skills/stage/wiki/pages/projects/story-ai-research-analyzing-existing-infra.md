# Story AI Research - Analyzing Existing Infra

## Summary

A research initiative to analyze and compare emerging decentralized AI infrastructure and compute projects, extracting architectural and economic insights to inform Story's own AI infrastructure development, particularly around data ownership, incentive design, IP monetization, and AI model usage in blockchain contexts.

## Claims

- The research objective is to analyze and compare emerging decentralized AI infrastructure and compute projects, extract core architectural and economic insights, and identify strategic takeaways that can inform the development and differentiation of Story's own AI infrastructure, particularly around data ownership, incentive design, IP monetization, and usage of AI model in the context of blockchain. `claim:claim_1_1` `confidence:1.00`
