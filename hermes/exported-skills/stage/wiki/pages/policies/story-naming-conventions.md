# Story Naming Convention

## Summary

Defines the naming scheme for Story's L1 blockchain networks (Testnet: ancient works/IP; Mainnet: ancient authors/Creators), domain reservations for public services, and network identifiers. Establishes consistent naming to avoid confusion across public-facing websites, RPC endpoints, and nodes.
