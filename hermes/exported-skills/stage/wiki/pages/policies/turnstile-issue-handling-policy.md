# Turnstile Issue Handling Policy

## Summary

Turnstile issues are deprioritized because they affect very few users, often due to automation or unnaturally fast usage. The engineering team focuses on critical bugs and new features like Payments, deeming it safe to ignore most turnstile complaints.
